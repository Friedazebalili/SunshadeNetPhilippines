Best Fit Guide

Not sure what service you need?

Send photos, measurements, or project details and we’ll help identify the best shade setup for your area.

  • For homes, start with residential shade or patio shade.
  • For vehicle protection, choose car park shade.
  • For guest spaces, choose resort or commercial shade.

Before we quote, prepare these details.

  • Photos of the area to be shaded
  • Estimated length and width of the space
