Who We Are

Your partner in practical shade solutions.

At Sunshade Net Philippines, we specialize in designing and installing sunshade net systems that help make outdoor spaces cooler, cleaner, and more usable.

Every project starts with understanding the actual space. We look at size, sun exposure, usage, structure needs, and the type of finish that will work best for the client.

Whether it is for a home, car park, patio, school, resort, or commercial property, our goal is to provide a shade solution that is functional, durable, and visually clean.
